BRGY. PINAGSAMA

An administrative division of Metro Manila in the Philippines. It is located in the western part of Taguig City.

Mission

We envision an esteemed and stable barangay of dynamic leadership, perfectly acquainted of their duties and responsibilities in command of a responsive local government, exhibiting a respectful attitude to duly constituted authorities, with moral ascendancy to lead, guided with godly conduct on virtues of harmonious relationship amongst constituent, morality and integrity as concrete example of concern, assuming more active roles in providing basic public and social services, towards attainment of a stable political, social and economic environment, imperative of people in pursuit to fulfillment of mutual goals and common aspirations.

Vision

To improve community governance, respect individual rights, and empower barangay officials. It aims to strengthen stakeholder involvement in economic development and quality of life. The goal is to maintain fair justice and ethical conduct among leaders. Discipline and integrity are cultivated, and the community is urged to work towards peace and order. The mission also aims to promote youth development through sports development. All constituents are encouraged to support local peace autonomy.
